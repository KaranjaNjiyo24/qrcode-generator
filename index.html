How It Works

  1. Paste a full URL (starting with http:// or https://) into the link field.
  2. Optionally upload your organization logo to place it at the center of the QR.
  3. Click Generate QR to render a high-quality preview in the preview card.
  4. Click Download PNG to save the generated QR code image.
